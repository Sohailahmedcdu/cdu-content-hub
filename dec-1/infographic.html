⚖️ Business Setup Steps

Step 1: Incorporate Business

Form corporation (federal or provincial) → Obtain business license

Step 2: Get OMVIC License

Apply for wholesale and/or export license (Ontario requirement)

Step 3: Open Business Bank Account

Provide incorporation docs, business plan, ID verification

Step 4: Register for HST

If revenue >$30K, register for HST with CRA

Step 5: Set Up Operations

Acquire Signal software, establish buyer network, launch marketing

✅ Vehicle Selection Criteria

  • Clean title (no liens, no salvage)
  • No flood damage or frame damage
  • No outstanding recalls (verify via service history)
  • 2-7 year old vehicles preferred
  • Mileage appropriate for market
  • Recent maintenance history
  • No modifications or non-standard parts

🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Operating without proper licensing
  • Purchasing flood-damaged or salvage vehicles
  • Underestimating HST obligations
  • Skipping vehicle inspections
  • Not verifying buyer legitimacy
  • Inadequate documentation and record-keeping
  • Shipping before payment clearance
